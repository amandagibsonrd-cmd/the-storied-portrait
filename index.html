Frequently Asked Questions

Do people need to be comfortable on camera?

No. Most people are not used to being filmed. The process is guided in a way that helps them settle in naturally.

Can family members be included?

Yes. Depending on the project, family members can be present, participate in conversation, or be included in portions of the filming.

What kinds of recipes work best for Recipe Capture?

Recipes with emotional connection work beautifully — holiday dishes, comfort foods, family traditions, and meals that carry memory.

How is the final film delivered?

Final films are delivered digitally so they can be saved, shared with family, and revisited for years to come.

Ready to preserve a story or family recipe?

Tell me a little about the person, the memory, or the recipe you want to capture, and I’ll help you choose the best fit.
