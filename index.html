About Amanda

The Storied Portrait began with a conversation.

What started as a simple conversation with my grandmother became something much deeper. I asked questions I thought I already knew the answers to, and heard stories I had never heard before.

That experience changed how I saw the people I love. It showed me how much of a person can remain hidden until someone makes space for their story.

The Storied Portrait was created to preserve those voices with warmth, cinematic beauty, and care.
